Our Story

Morning Kick Cafe started with a straightforward mission: to provide great coffee, satisfying food, and a dependable spot for the Macquarie Fields community to refuel.

Built for the Community

Based right near Glenquarie Town Centre, we see firsthand how fast-paced mornings can get. Whether you are racing to catch your morning train, dropping the kids off at school, or looking for a comfortable corner to study with a hot drink, we wanted to build a space that fits into your daily routine seamlessly.

Fresh Food, Fast Pace

We do not believe in cutting corners just because you are in a rush. From our morning espresso to our loaded burgers, big breakfasts, and sweet treats, our kitchen keeps things fresh and moving fast so you can get on with your day.

A Place for Everyone

At the core of Morning Kick is a local team that loves serving our neighbors. We are proud to be a family-friendly venue where you can bring the kids for lunch, catch up with friends, or just grab a quick takeaway bite before work. Come on in and let us help you start your day right.
